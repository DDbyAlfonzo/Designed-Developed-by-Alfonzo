Tax evasion is a global issue, and the Tax Reporting Tool aims to promote cooperation and information exchange between tax administrations to combat it.

Capitec W.A.R.M

WhatsApp for Relationship Managers is a Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed to streamline communication between Relationship Managers and their bankers. It enables Relationship Managers to efficiently close, refer, or escalate banker requests.

FNB

nav Car on the FNB app allows users to buy and sell vehicles, renew licence discs plus track its delivery. You can pay for vehicle fines, and much more, all in one convenient place.

Discovery - Vitality

Discovery Vitality is a programme designed to inspire healthier lifestyle choices by rewarding members for taking steps to improve their health.

Vikela website

A central platform where users can design, customise, and order their unique smart safe for various sectors.

Vikela web portal

Providing users with the ability to customise, monitor, and add devices to their smart safe, print receipts and count the bills deposited.

Huawei tracking system

The Huawei tracking system enables users to monitor miners and vehicle activity on mining sites. It can also be adapted to track the spread and outbreaks of the COVID-19 virus.

Pro-Find

The Pro-Find mobile application is designed for SME businesses and job seekers in South Africa. It streamlines the scanning, shortlisting, and hiring process, making it faster and more efficient.

Quick score pro

QSP (Quick Score Pro) is a mobile app that enables users to capture, manage, and view match and shooting range scores, based on their user roles.
